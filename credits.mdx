# Credits & Pricing

Edge uses a credit-based pricing model. Every API call costs a set number of credits. No monthly minimums, no subscriptions required.

## Credit costs per service

| Service | Endpoint | Credits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| IBAN Validation (Full) | `/services/iban/validate/{iban}` | 3 |
| IBAN Validation (Basic) | `/services/iban/validate-basic/{iban}` | 1 |
| IBAN Bulk Validation | `/services/iban/bulk-validate` | 2 per IBAN |
| Sanctions Screening | `/services/sanctions/screen` | 5 |
| Sanctions Stats | `/services/sanctions/stats` | Free |
| Exchange Rates (Latest) | `/services/fx/latest` | 1 |
| Exchange Rates (Historical) | `/services/fx/historical/{date}` | 1 |
| Currency Conversion | `/services/fx/convert` | 1 |
| Currency List | `/services/fx/currencies` | 1 |
| BIC/SWIFT Lookup | `/services/bank/bic/{bic}` | 1 |
| Bank Directory | `/services/bank/country/{country}` | 1 |
| Bank Search | `/services/bank/search` | 1 |
| Bank Countries | `/services/bank/countries` | 1 |
| CR Lookup | `/services/cr/{country}/lookup/{crNumber}` | 10 |
| CR Search | `/services/cr/{country}/search` | 3 |
| Country Lookup | `/services/country/{code}` | 1 |
| Country List | `/services/country/list` | 1 |
| Country Search | `/services/country/search` | 1 |
| Email Validation | `/services/email/validate/{email}` | 1 |
| Phone Validation | `/services/phone/validate/{phone}` | 1 |

## Free tier

Every new account gets **500 free credits** — no credit card required. That's enough for:
- 166 full IBAN validations
- 500 exchange rate lookups
- 100 sanctions screenings
- Or any combination of services

## Buying more credits

Purchase credit packs from your dashboard under **Billing**. Credits never expire.

## Plans

| Feature | Free | Pay-as-you-go | Monthly | Annual |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| API Keys | 1 | 5 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Team Members | 1 | 1 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Rate Limit | 10/min | 60/min | 200/min | 200/min |
| Log Retention | 7 days | 30 days | 90 days | 90 days |
| Overage | No | No | Yes | Yes |

Monthly and annual plans include **10,000 credits per month**. If you exceed your allocation, overage credits are charged at $0.005/credit (monthly) or $0.004/credit (annual).

## Checking your balance

- **Dashboard**: Your credit balance is shown in the top-right corner
- **API**: `GET /organizations/{org}/credits` returns your current balance
