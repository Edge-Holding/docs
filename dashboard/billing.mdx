# Billing

## Credit balance

Your credit balance is visible in the dashboard header and on the **Billing** page. Credits are deducted per API call based on the service used.

## Buying credits

Purchase credit packs from the **Billing** page. Credits are added to your balance immediately and never expire.

## Subscription plans

| Plan | Credits/Month | Rate Limit | Overage Rate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | 10,000 | 200/min | $0.005/credit |
| Annual | 10,000 | 200/min | $0.004/credit |

Subscription plans include 10,000 credits per month. If you exceed your allocation, additional usage is charged at the overage rate.

## Managing your subscription

Click **Manage Subscription** on the Billing page to access the customer portal where you can:

- Update payment method
- View invoices
- Change or cancel your plan

## Free tier

Every new account starts with **500 free credits**. No credit card required. When they run out, purchase a credit pack or subscribe to a plan.
