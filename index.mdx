## Why Edge?

- **12 services, one key** — IBAN, sanctions, FX, BIC, bank directory, CR, country data, email, phone, and more.
- **Pay per call** — No subscriptions required. Credit-based pricing starting at $0.006 per call.
- **Fast** — Most responses under 50ms.
- **110+ countries** — IBAN validation covers 110+ countries with full bank details.
- **1.2M+ entities** — Sanctions screening against OFAC, UN, and UK OFSI databases.
- **500 free credits** — Start building immediately, no credit card required.
